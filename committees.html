Service & Stewardship

Our Committees

Dedicated teams ensuring that every area of church life — from finances to outreach — is run with excellence and integrity.

Get Involved

Committees of Bethesda

Each committee welcomes members who want to serve. Contact the church office to find out where your gifts fit best.

🏦

Finance

The Finance committee oversees the financial health of the church, ensuring proper stewardship of all resources. This includes budgeting, financial reporting, risk management, and the annual audit processes, working closely with the Finance Steward and the broader leadership.

🏛

Trust Properties

The Trust Properties committee is responsible for overseeing the church's trust assets and property, ensuring long-term sustainability and proper legal governance in collaboration with the Board of Trustees and legal advisors.

💧

Fountain of Healing

Fountain of Healing is at the heart of our pastoral care mission, providing support to those experiencing illness, bereavement, and hardship. The association also hosts the Annual Charity Golf Day.

📡

Communication & Media

The Communication & Media committee manages all internal and external communications, including social media, digital ministry, church notices, and media production for online worship services on Facebook and YouTube.

🎓

Education & Bursary

The Education & Bursary committee supports the educational aspirations of members and community youth through bursary assistance and mentorship, recognising education as a key tool for community flourishing.

💼

Entrepreneurship

The Entrepreneurship committee supports economic empowerment by helping community members develop business skills, access resources, and build sustainable livelihoods through workshops and mentorship programmes.

📊

Monitoring & Evaluation

This committee ensures that all mission and outreach programmes are assessed for effectiveness and impact, so the church can continually improve its service to the community and steward its resources wisely.

⚖

Governance

The Governance committee ensures that Bethesda operates with integrity, accountability, and transparency — overseeing constitutional compliance and organisational best practice in line with the MCSA's polity.

🌟

Special Projects

The Special Projects committee coordinates key fundraising events and community impact initiatives. These projects raise vital funds to support church operations, outreach, and the flagship feeding scheme.
