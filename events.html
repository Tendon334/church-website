Notice · July – November 2026

Rhona 2026 — Themes & Conveners

  • Rhona will run from July 2026 to November 2026. There will be two Rhona celebrations per month (mid-month and month-end).
  • The opening Rhona celebration will be led by the Bethesda Leadership on Sunday, 12 July 2026.
  • Each Rhona celebration will be led by a Champion from the Cell Cluster Groups or any other designated groups.
  • Each Rhona celebration will run for a maximum of 20 minutes to allow sufficient time for the Sermon.
  • Each Cell group will receive its individual target through the Cell Leadership.
