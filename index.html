Welcome Home

A Century of Faith & Service

Founded in 1904 as Berea Methodist Church, Bethesda Methodist Mission today serves a congregation of approximately 850 members and a growing online community. The name "Bethesda" — meaning place of healing — reflects our vision of outreach, restoration, and community impact.
